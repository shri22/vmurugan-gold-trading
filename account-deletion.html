Account & Data Deletion Request

Your Privacy Matters

At VMurugan Jewellery, we respect your right to privacy and data control. You can request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time. This page provides clear instructions on how to submit a deletion request.

How to Request Account & Data Deletion

To delete your account and personal data, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Send an Email

Email Address: vmuruganjewellers@gmail.com

Subject Line: Account Deletion Request – VMurugan Jewellery

Step 2: Include Required Information

In your email, please provide:

  • Your registered phone number
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
Step 3: Wait for Confirmation

Processing Time: Your request will be processed within 7 working days.

You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been successfully deleted.

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon processing your deletion request, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Phone number
  • MPIN (Mobile PIN)
  • OTP login history
  • Profile information
  • Account activity logs
  • Saved preferences
  • All backend account records

Data That May Be Retained Temporarily

Important: For security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance purposes, non-personal system logs (such as IP addresses and access timestamps) may be retained for 30–90 days after account deletion. These logs do not contain personally identifiable information and will be automatically purged after the retention period.
